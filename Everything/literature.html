Additional literature

However, this doesn't mean I agree with every sentence in every book, but you can learn a lot from these books. Just don't forget to think for yourself, keep asking yourself: What does this mean for you?; How do you experience this? The books vary from neuroscientists till a scripture from the Buddha, read it with an open mind and you'll find wisdom in every piece.
